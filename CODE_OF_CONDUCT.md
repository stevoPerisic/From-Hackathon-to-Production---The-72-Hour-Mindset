# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent.
- Give actionable feedback.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing or intimidation.
- Disruptive or bad-faith participation.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions
that violate this Code of Conduct.
